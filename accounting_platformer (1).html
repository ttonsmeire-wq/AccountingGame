How to Play

Controls

  • Tap / Click / Space / ↑ — Jump
  • Double-tap quickly — Double jump
  • World scrolls right automatically

Hazards — level resets to last checkpoint, no life lost

  • Gaps — fall in to reset
  • Floor spikes — touch to reset
  • Ceiling spikes — appear from level 4 onward
  • Moving platforms — time your jump

Checkpoints & Gate

  • Levels 1–5: one checkpoint midway through
  • Levels 6–8: two checkpoints
  • Levels 9–10: four checkpoints
  • Wrong checkpoint answer → lose a life, retry from that checkpoint
  • Reach the gate → level complete!
  • ★ Bonus CPs (levels 4, 7 & 10): answer a Hard bonus question for an extra life!
